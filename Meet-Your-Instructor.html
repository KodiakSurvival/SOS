Outdoorsman

Ben Dent is an accomplished outdoorsman, life-long fisherman, and has proven knowlege in every aspect of survival.  Serving as a US Coast Guard Rescue Swimmer has preapred him for meeting challenges and overcoming them, through determination and fortitude.  

While living in Kodiak, Alaska, Ben has sharpened his skills fishing for King Salmon, Coho, and other salmon species.  When hunting or fishing, he is respectful of the land, the native peoples and other Alaskans with whom he comes into contact.  Ben expresses his positive attitude and collaborative approach in everything he does.
