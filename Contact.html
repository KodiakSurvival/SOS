"I attended Ben Dent's School of Survival and it paid off..."

-Mr. R. Jones

While 4-wheeling with his family Mr. Jones became disoriented and found himself driving too long in the wrong direction in a frantic attempt to rejoin his group.  

Jones, luckily had attended the School of Survival course on Survival Basics, and he quickly realized he needed to assess his fuel situation, collect freshwater from a little stream he discovered, and built a fire to (if necessary) spend the night in the outback, hoping to reconnect with his party or find a road or trail in the morning.

By following the tips and know-how he received from School of Survival, Jones stayed warm and more importantly stayed dry, and in the first light of morning he made it down to a clearing where he was spotted.
